Introducing the Liverpool FC Home Football Shorts 2024-25!
Channel Your Inner Red: Liverpool F.C. Home Football Shorts 2024-25.

Be a part of the team with the official Liverpool F.C. Home Football Shorts for adults and kids.

Replicate the look of your Anfield heroes with these performance-ready shorts, designed to keep you cool and comfortable on the pitch or in the stands.

Built for the beautiful game, these shorts feature Nike’s innovative Dri-FIT ADV technology.

This moisture-wicking fabric draws sweat away from your skin, keeping you dry and focused throughout the match.

The lightweight polyester construction offers exceptional breathability and freedom of movement, allowing you to perform at your peak.

Inspired by the club’s rich heritage, the shorts boast the iconic Liverpool F.C. crest on one leg, alongside the instantly recognizable Nike Swoosh on the other.

These shorts are more than just clothing items:

They represent your unwavering support for the Reds!

The bold red colorway with yellow and white side panels reflects the club’s classic home kit design, unveiled for the 2024-25 season.

More than just team colours, these shorts are crafted with sustainability in mind.

As part of Liverpool’s commitment to “The Red Way,” the shorts are made from 100% recycled polyester fabric.

This eco-friendly material is derived from recycled plastic bottles, minimizing environmental impact without compromising on performance.

Whether you’re a die-hard Reds supporter or a budding footballer, the Liverpool F.C. Home Football Shorts 2024-25 are a must-have addition to your kit.
